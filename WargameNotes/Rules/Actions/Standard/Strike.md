> [!Warning] Restrictions
> - Activating unit must be [[Engaged]].
> - Must target an enemy unit that it is [[Engaged]] with.

> [!info] Strike Standard Action
> When a unit performs a Strike [[Standard Action]] that unit attacks with any/all weapons that are [[Eligible to Attack]] or have the [[Melee|Melee Weapon Trait]].
