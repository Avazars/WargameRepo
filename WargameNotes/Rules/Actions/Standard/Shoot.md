> [!Warning] Restrictions
> - Cannot target a unit that is [[Engaged]].
> - Must be able to Draw LoS to Target Unit.

> [!info] Strike Standard Action
> When a unit performs a Shoot [[Standard Action]] that unit targets an enemy unit and performs a Ranged Attack with any/all weapons that are [[Eligible to Attack]]. If this unit is not able to see a majority of the targeted unit then the targeted unit gains the [[Obscured|Obscured Status]].
