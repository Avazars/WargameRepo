> [!Warning] Restrictions
> - Activating unit must be within range of a [[Point of Interest]].

> [!info] Claim Objective
> When a unit performs a Claim [[Standard Action]] if that unit is within range of a [[Point of Interest]] and that unit has a higher [[Control]] value then any other unit at the end of the next objective phase, that unit [[Claiming a POI|Claims]] that [[Point of Interest]].
