A unique test is where both players roll 6d6 and count the number of unique values in the pool that they rolled.

> [!EXAMPLE] Example Unique test
> 1. Both players roll 6d6.
> 2. Player one rolls: {2,2,**3**,4,4,**6**} obtaining 2 unique values from their pool. 
> 3. Player two rolls: {**1**,2,2,2,3,3} obtaining 1 unique value from their pool.
> 
> Thus Player one wins because they have 2 unique values while player two only has 1.
> 
> *Note : {1,2,3,4,5,6} would be the best roll possible with a unique test.*
