When reading this guide its important to know what different shorthand means and how it will be used in context of the rules text.

### Shorthand

| Shorthand | Meaning                                                                                                                                                       |
| --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Dice      | Whenever we are talking about dice we are talking about 6 sided dice, unless specified specifically.                                                          |
| `+/- #D`  | This is how we denote a bonus or a penalty to a dice pool when rolling, it means that you add `+` or subtract `-` an amount `#` of dice `D` from a dice pool. |
| LoS       | This is denote the words "Line of Sight" and just means that any part of the unit can see another                                                             |
