> [!warning] Trigger
> **Gains**
A unit gains the Cover Status when that unit performs the [[Take Cover]] [[Standard Action]].
>
> **Looses**
> A unit looses the Cover Status when a majority of the unit moves outside of 1" of a [[Terrain Feature]].

> [!info] Cover
> While a unit has the Cover Status: 
> - That unit gets (+3)[[ARM]] against shooting attacks that cannot draw [[LOS]] to a majority of the unit.
