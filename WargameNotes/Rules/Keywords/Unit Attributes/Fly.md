> [!Note] Fly
> When a unit with the [[Fly]] keyword moves it may spend 2" of movement to gain the following benefits until the end of the battle round:
>  - Ignore [[Terrain Effects ]] when moving and treat all [[Terrain]] as having a height of 0".
>  - Gain +1 to their [[Agility|AGI]].
